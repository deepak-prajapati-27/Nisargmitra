Perfume Grade Alcohol

The following is a general guide to the levels of fragrance concentrate found in Purenso Perfume Grade Alcohol Based.

  • Fragrance : 15 - 25% Fragrance Oil
  • Eau De Parfum : 8 - 15% Fragrance Oil
  • Eau De Toilette : 4 - 10% Fragrance Oil
  • Eau De Cologne : 2 - 5% Fragrance Oil
  • Aftershave/ Splash : < 3% Fragrance Oil

Below is a guide to the naming protocal associated with fragrance sprays

Fragrance, Parfum Or Extrait

  • Between 15-25% Fragrance oil.
  • Parfum, also known as extrait de parfum or pure fragrance, has the highest fragrance concentration.
  • Lasts the longest of all, usually 8 to 24 hours.
  • Not always suitable for users with dry or sensitive skin. The most expensive of all scents, Slightly thicker and oilier.

Eau De Perfume

  • Between 8-15% Fragrance oil.
  • One of the most common fragrance types and is suitable for veryday wear contains 15 - 20% pure fragrance essence.
  • Lasts for about 5 to 8 hours

Eau De Toilette

  • Between 4-10% Fragrance oil.
  • A little spray composition with 4 - 10% pure fragrance essence dissolved in Purenso pure fragrance essence.
  • Usually lasts for about 3 hours.

Eau De Cologne

  • Between 2-5% Fragrance oil.
  • The oldest term for fragrance, this concentration is the most diluted.
  • Usually lasts for about 2 hours.
  • Usually sold in sprays, Eau De Cologne is normally marketed to the youth markets.
